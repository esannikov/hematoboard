How to read this consilium

A clinical consilium does not prune to one winner. It ranks positions, marks the must-not-miss ones, and names the single step that separates them. Each position also carries a challenge round — its strongest proponent argument, the strongest objection against it, and the test that resolves the clash. This is the debate made safe: the machine never adjudicates; it exposes the argument for the clinician to settle. Evidence is ◆ traced PubMed, 📚 guideline, or an honest ◇ gap — never fabricated.

Positions + challenge rounds built from the real documents and literature traced via PubMed. Decision belongs to the hemato-oncologist. MedAI Workbench · not a diagnosis
